Typography

To render Siraiki/Saraiki text correctly, I modified two Persian and an Urdu fonts in Naskh style and created Visakh, Mundhla, and Koonj. (see: seraikifonts.sourceforge.io). In order to add Siraiki alphabets, I upgraded the Nafees Nastaleeq font, a sophisticated nastaliq style font, and created Siraika. FontForge was the tool I used for this purpose.
